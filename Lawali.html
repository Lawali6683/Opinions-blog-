In this review, Lawali Haruna Gusau explores the transformative impact of technological innovations on agricultural growth in Nigeria. **The Role of Government Policy in Agricultural Growth: Perspectives from Lawali Haruna Gusau**

Lawali Haruna Gusau offers a critical analysis of recent Nigerian government policies aimed at boosting agricultural growth. He discusses the effectiveness of subsidies, the impact of import restrictions on local farming, and the strategic importance of agricultural education and training in empowering farmers and increasing production efficiency.

**Lawali Haruna Gusau on Enhancing Agricultural Productivity through Cooperative Farming**

In this narrative, Lawali Haruna Gusau discusses the role of cooperative farming in enhancing agricultural productivity in Nigeria. He points out how cooperatives help in pooling resources, accessing modern technologies, and negotiating better terms in markets, thus leading to improved yields and profitability for smallholder farmers.
